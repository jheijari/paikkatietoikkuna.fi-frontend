The Finnish geoportal Paikkatietoikkuna has been renewed. You can proceed to the map service by clicking the link in the yellow box, or trough the following link https://kartta.paikkatietoikkuna.fi. Other contents, such as INSPIRE guidance, can now be found on the website of National Land Survey of Finland. You can also access these pages through the links in the blue box.

All existing user accounts have been moved to the new service, but the passwords have been cleared. You can get a new password here, if you are entering the service for the first time. If you encounter problems on this site, please contact our customer service: paikkatietoikkuna@maanmittauslaitos.fi

In the map service you can for example view spatial data by different organizations, create thematic maps or publish an embedded map on your website.
